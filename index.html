P1 Arrow Placement Simulator

practice your arrow spots

How to play
  • Press Start — your two arrow debuffs will appear.
  • Matching arrows (same direction) → place both in the cardinal box (mid-edge).
  • Mixed arrows (different directions) → place both in the intercardinal box (corner).
  • Click your 5s arrow spot first, then your 8s arrow spot before the timers run out.
  • Any wrong click ends the round immediately.
